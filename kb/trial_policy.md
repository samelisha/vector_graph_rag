# Free Trial Policy

## Trial Duration
The standard free trial duration is 14 days unless otherwise specified at sign-up.

## Trial Conversion
At the end of the trial, the account automatically converts to a paid subscription unless cancelled prior to conversion.

## Trial Limitations
Some features may be limited during the trial period. Usage caps may apply.

## Credit Card Requirement
Some regions require a valid payment method to begin a trial, while others allow cardless trials.

## Abuse Prevention
Multiple trial creation for the same user, company, or payment method may result in trial termination.
