# Billing FAQ

## When are invoices generated?
Invoices are generated at the start of each billing cycle and are available for download from the account dashboard.

## What happens if payment fails?
If payment fails:
1. You receive an automated email notification
2. We retry the payment up to 3 times over 7 days
3. The account may be moved to Suspended status

## What payment methods are supported?
We currently support major credit cards, some debit cards, and select digital wallets depending on country of residence.

## Can I change my billing currency?
Billing currency is determined at account creation based on location and cannot typically be changed later.

## Are taxes included?
Taxes such as VAT or GST may be added depending on your billing address and applicable regulations.
