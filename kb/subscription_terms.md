# Subscription Terms

## Overview
Subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing cycle unless cancelled. Users are notified by email 7 days before renewal where required by law.

## Billing Cycle
The billing cycle may be monthly, quarterly, or annual depending on the selected plan at purchase time.

## Renewal
Subscriptions renew automatically using the default payment method saved in the account.

## Grace Period
Where applicable, there is a 3-day grace period during which payment failures may be retried before access is suspended.

## Eligibility for Services
Access to subscription services is active only while the subscription status is marked as Active. Accounts in Suspended or Cancelled status may lose access to premium features.

## Changes to Terms
We may update these terms periodically. Updates are communicated via email or on the account dashboard.
