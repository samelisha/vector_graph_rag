# Refund Policy

## Refund Eligibility
Refunds may be issued for charges incurred within the past 7 calendar days if the service has not been substantially used.

## Processing Time
Refund processing takes between 5 and 10 business days depending on the payment provider.

## Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be provided in cases of service interruption, duplicate billing, or documented technical issues preventing usage.

## Non-Refundable Items
The following are non-refundable:
- promotional plans
- discounted annual plans after 30 days
- one-time setup fees

## How to Request a Refund
To request a refund, contact Support through the Help Center or open a billing ticket within the account dashboard.

## Chargebacks
If a chargeback is initiated with the bank, the subscription will be suspended while the dispute is investigated.

## Regional Differences
Some jurisdictions require a longer refund window (for example, 14 days in the EU). In such cases, regional laws override internal refund timelines.
